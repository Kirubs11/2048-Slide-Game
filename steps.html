2048 SLIDE GAME

HOW TO PLAY ?


1. When you first open the game, you'll see a 5x5 grid with two tiles that contain the number 2.

2. You can move the tiles in four directions - up, down, left, and right . To move the tiles,
you can use your arrow keys on your keyboard or swipe on your mobile device.

3. Each time you move the tiles, a new tile will appear on the board with a value of either 2 or 4.

4. If two tiles with the same number collide with each other, they will merge into one tile with the sum of their
values.For example, if slide a tile with a value of 2 into another tile with a value of 2,
they will merge to become a single tile with a value of 4.

5. The goal of the game is to create a tile with a value of 2048 However,
even if you don't reach that goal, you can continue playing to try and beat your high score.

6. The game is over when there are no more possible moves on the board,
or when you reach the goal of 2048.

7. To restart the game, simply refresh the page or click the "New Game" button.

8. Keep in mind that the key to success in this game is to think ahead and plan your moves carefully.
Don't make random moves, or you may quickly find yourself running out of options.
